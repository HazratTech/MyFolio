# Motion Design

Everything uses Framer Motion.

- Staggered fade-ups as sections enter the viewport.
- Smooth text reveals with slight upward motion.
- Hover lift (6–8px) on cards with a soft shadow.
- Animated counters for business stats.
- Magnetic hover effect on primary CTA buttons.
- Hero chat demo with sequential typing, message bubbles, and status changes.
- Integration lines that draw in as the section becomes visible.
- FAQ items with smooth height animations.
- Subtle parallax on the hero illustration (mouse movement only on desktop).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` and keep animations fast (200–500ms).

Avoid:
- Heavy particle backgrounds.
- Infinite floating animations everywhere.
- Overly dramatic page transitions.
- Excessive blur or glowing effects.
